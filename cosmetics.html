Cosmetics

The cosmetics industry brings unique supply chain challenges. Omni-channel distribution and market localisation are crucial to business success. It is also necessary for brands to understand the prevailing market trends to improve business.
There is a need to trace the products and ensure they are not damaged. It helps prevent wastage by tracking the points in the supply chain that something went wrong. It is also essential to introduce attractive schemes and analyse them in real-time to stay ahead of peers.

Solution

Eazy DMS is the ideal solution for the cosmetics industry to ensure real-time tracking of the products and SKUs. It can also provide actionable data for the sales team to make informed decisions.

Benefits from our solution

The solution provides complete control over the distribution channel. It allows proper SKU mapping with centralisation of data. It can ensure proper order management and prevent stock-out conditions.
Eazy DMS can track the performance of the supply chain components and provide information about the proceeds from each product and size variants, etc. The sales team can introduce targeted loyalty schemes and track them in real-time to stay ahead of their peers.
