Auto components and Lubricants

The industry is plagued by various problems like the optimal routing of components and ensuring supplies in time. There is also a need to have a buffer inventory to meet seasonal demand. The distributors and the sales team find it challenging to start the vast array of products and varying SKUs.
The manufacturers emphasise just in time supplies that focus more on having a digitised supply chain. The complex supply chain requires the sales team to have a comprehensive view of the stocks present in the warehouse.

Solution

The first step towards a robust supply chain workflow is to digitise it. Eazy DMS does much more! It allows you to address the challenges of having multiple products from different brands. The system can support Multi-brand invoices.

Benefits from our solution

The solution is pre-integrated with Tally and Busy, allowing real-time sales information transfer. It also allows the sales to have a comprehensive overview of sales information of the numerous products with details on the variants and SKU details.
The order management process is smoothened, and there are automated alerts to prevent stock-outs. Moreover, it allows proper replenishment of stocks to adhere to just in time workflows that the industry follows.
