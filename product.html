About Us

We have over 14 years of experience

Fusion DMS has travelled many miles i.e from integration with your Distributor’s Tally & Busy & providing complete control over the distributor's billing Software with the same flavor of Portal based DMS.

Eazy’s re-engineered Fusion DMS provides a comprehensive solution for end-to-end distribution channel management, equipping not just Superstockists and Distributors but Retailers, Influencers, Managers, Sales Person, Support Centers along the way as well.

By bringing everything under a single cohesive system, something that Hybrid DMS failed to do, the Fusion DMS enables businesses to establish multi-dimensional control & accessibility across different processes of their distribution channel. As such, the Fusion DMS overcomes the limitations of distributor management and redefines how modern businesses approach distributor & loyalty management in three ways – streamlining connectivity, establishing seamless communication and facilitating collaboration.

Update to Eazy's Hybrid DMS 3.0 is Now!

EazyDMS 4.0 or Fusion DMS stands out as a game-changing innovation where distributor management systems are concerned. By establishing a holistic management ecosystem of the entire distribution channel, the Fusion DMS has turned itself into a powerful & scalable demonstration of mobile-based, all-in-one DMS that brings four different apps together, integrates loyalty management, and delivers unimaginable connectivity and control among people and processes. By implementing Eazy’s Fusion DMS 4.0, businesses can easily & effectively connect, communicate & collaborate across their entire distribution channel.

Implementation/Rollout Model

Our aim is not only to provide the Next Gen of Hybrid Distributor Management System, but also to provide the best experience and to achieve maximum rollouts. We provide a dedicated Account Manager who takes care of the progress of the project timely rollouts leading to successful implementation of DMS. We are tied up with an extensive network of Tally Partners who would physically visit your Distributors/Dealers and train them about the software. This way we have been able to increase the efficiency and successfully accomplish 400 rollouts in a month.

Support/Helpdesk Model

Our work doesn’t end with installation DMS (Distributor Management System). We want you to take maximum result out of our DMS which requires hand holding. To cater to the day-to-day queries of the Distributor/Dealer on how to use the system, Eazy DMS provides you with a dedicated helpdesk resources who become the single point of contact for all the issues and coordination for your Distributor/Dealer network related to Tally, Busy or your distributor Management System.
