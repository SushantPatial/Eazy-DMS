Stationery

The industry manufactures many stationery items, and the sales team must track sales information across the country. It is essential to use a robust DMS system to track such a considerable volume of data.
The industry must prepare for demand spikes and utilise historical information to predict them. The distribution channel can stretch far, so the sales team must collect orders online.

Solution

Eazy DMS provides several features that ensure improved productivity of the sales team. It can track detailed sales information and allows the sales managers to drill down to the minutest detail. It is easier to track the performance of channel components in real-time.

Benefits from our solution

Our solution improves the mobility of the sales team as orders can now be taken online. Hence, the order management process is smoothened, and there are no risks of missed orders. Distributors can now enter sales details online without any duplication of work.
The sales team can have a clear overview of the sales status of all products and the SKUs in real-time. The system can integrate with Tally and Busy for faster forwarding order-related information. Eazy DMS ensures trust for multi-brand distributors.
