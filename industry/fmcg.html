FMCG

FMCG companies find it challenging to increase adequate market coverage mainly due to the absence of real-time sales data. The industry also faces a lack of visibility into work orders that limit the availability of actionable analytics.
Specific product lines are also plagued by wastage. The companies also find it difficult to track the wastage levels and the reasons. The distribution system must be agile, which most entities cannot handle.

Solution

The solution to the industry problems is Eazy DMS. It has seamless integration with Tally and supports tracking sales information across the primary, secondary and tertiary levels. It also allows data centralisation and ensures flexibility in the order management system.

Benefits from our solution

Eazy DMS has helped digitise our FMCG clients' entire distribution chain. Now our clients receive secondary sales data from the distributors in real-time. There is no duplication of work. The solution allows maintaining optimum levels and ensures faster stock replenishment.
The implementation of Eazy DMS ensures the distribution channel operations are streamlined. It provides enhanced distribution planning to minimise additional costs due to wastage and damage. Moreover, it also ensures flexibility in handling purchase orders to address fluctuating demand adequately.
