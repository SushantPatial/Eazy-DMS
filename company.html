Training

In order to survive the intense global competition, industries are under a constant pressure to streamline their production, buying and selling operations. The growing needs for functional integration are compelling businesses to look for a solution which can streamline their operations, improve productivity and boost profits. This is where ERP comes into picture, it not only streamlines the processes but also leads to automation, asking for the minimum human interventions for prescribed events and procedures.

ERP being a vast concept demands a lot of consulting before its implementation. Being an ERP expert, you get the opportunity to view an organization as a whole, visualize improvements, efficiencies and see your suggestions work in a better performing organization. Interaction with top management and decision makers of the industry leads to an enormous hands on learning experience, which instils several important professional as well as technical skills.

We help you become Process Experts!

"Things taught are better learnt"

Our ERP assignments involve the interplay of various disciplines such as finance, production, distribution, sales, procurement, stores, budgeting and planning. The average compensation for ERP consultants have been above the rest of the computer specialty fields, which in itself makes it an attractive career option.

Training Objectives

  •   Let the trainees develop a clear understanding of the basics of networking and installation of hardware and software concepts.
  •   Instilling technical, functional area and end-user level knowledge of ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and CRM (Customer Relationship Management).
  •   Implementation of knowledge and skills gained in practical (live) environment.
  •   Assessment of knowledge and skill competencies gained.
  •   Transforming trainees into ERP experts.
  •   Absorbing qualified trainees.

On the Job Training and Live Assignments

Our on the job training concept helps the trainees work and learn in the practical environment. It lets them face the practical challenges and evolve into a true expert.

Get the Professional Edge

The training provided at 3S is not like any other ERP training, rather it is a training full of actual case studies, where trainees get a chance to face the actual business process challenges and find ways to counter the same. Our training process acts as a catalyzing agent which transformations the trainees into ERP experts and gain a professional edge.

Earn while you Learn

Candidates are hired as trainees, who are later (after training completion) absorbed in our system itself. Trainees are entitled for a stipend while they are pursuing the training. Once the training gets completed an assessment is conducted, after which the qualified candidates are directly absorbed in our system.
